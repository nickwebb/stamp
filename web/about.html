About Stamp

Our Mission

Stamp was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to create a music ecosystem that truly serves artists and fans. We believe the current music industry is broken, with artists receiving pennies for their work and fans disconnected from the creators they love.

Our platform is designed to flip this model on its head, creating direct connections between artists and their communities while ensuring fair compensation and ownership for creators.

Our Story

Stamp began in 2023 when a group of musicians, technologists, and music lovers came together with a shared frustration about the state of the music industry. We saw talented artists struggling to make ends meet despite millions of streams, and fans who wanted to support their favorite artists but lacked meaningful ways to do so.

We asked ourselves: What if we could build a platform that prioritizes the artist-fan relationship and creates a sustainable ecosystem for music to thrive?
