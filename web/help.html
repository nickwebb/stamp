Frequently Asked Questions

How is Stamp different from other music platforms?

Stamp is built on a community-first model where artists retain ownership of their work and receive fair compensation. Unlike traditional streaming platforms, we prioritize direct artist-fan relationships and provide tools for artists to build sustainable careers without giving up their rights or the majority of their earnings.

How do artists get paid on Stamp?

Artists on Stamp earn revenue through multiple streams: direct fan support (tips, subscriptions, and purchases), streaming royalties (which are significantly higher than industry standards), and community participation rewards. All payments are transparent, with artists receiving at least 85% of all revenue generated by their content.

Can I use Stamp on my mobile device?

Yes! Stamp is available as a web app and native applications for iOS and Android devices. You can stream music, support artists, and engage with the community from any device.
