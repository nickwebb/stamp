Getting Started

1

Create Your Artist Profile

Your profile is your home base on Stamp. Make it stand out with high-quality images, a compelling bio, and links to your social media.

Pro Tip

Use a professional profile photo and cover image that represent your brand. Your bio should tell your story in an authentic way that connects with potential fans.

2

Upload Your Music

Share your tracks with the world. Stamp supports high-quality audio formats to ensure your music sounds its best.

Pro Tip

Upload in WAV or FLAC format for the best sound quality. Add compelling artwork and detailed track information to help your music stand out.

3

Set Up Your Revenue Streams

Configure how you want to monetize your music. Options include streaming, direct support, subscriptions, and merchandise.

Pro Tip

Diversify your revenue streams. Many successful artists on Stamp earn from a combination of streaming, fan subscriptions, and direct support.

4

Connect With Your Community

Engage with fans through updates, exclusive content, and direct messaging. Building relationships is key to success on Stamp.

Pro Tip

Respond to comments and messages promptly. Share behind-the-scenes content and updates about your creative process to keep fans engaged.

Building Your Audience

Consistent Releases

Regular releases keep your audience engaged and help you stay relevant in the algorithm.

Community Engagement

Respond to comments, host Q&A sessions, and make your fans feel valued.

Exclusive Content

Offer special perks to your subscribers, like early access to new music or behind-the-scenes content.

Cross-Promotion

Collaborate with other artists on Stamp to reach new audiences and create exciting content.

Monetization Options

Streaming Revenue

Earn from every stream of your music. Stamp pays significantly higher rates than traditional streaming platforms.

  • 85% of streaming revenue goes directly to artists
  • Transparent payment structure
  • Monthly payouts with detailed analytics

Fan Subscriptions

Offer subscription tiers to your most dedicated fans in exchange for exclusive benefits.

  • Set your own subscription prices
  • Offer different tiers with varying perks
  • Create a stable monthly income

Direct Support

Fans can support you directly through tips, one-time payments, or purchasing individual tracks.

  • No minimum threshold for withdrawals
  • Multiple payment options for fans
  • Personalized thank-you messages

Merchandise

Sell physical and digital merchandise directly to your fans through your Stamp profile.

  • Integrated merch store
  • Print-on-demand options available
  • Digital goods like stems and samples
