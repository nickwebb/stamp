Our Key Differences

Fair Compensation

We've built a payment model that ensures artists receive the majority of revenue generated from their work. With 85% of all earnings going directly to creators, we're setting a new standard for fair compensation in the industry.

Full Ownership

Artists on Stamp maintain 100% ownership of their music. We believe creators should control their work and how it's distributed. Our platform is designed to help you share your content while protecting your rights.

Community-Driven

Stamp is built around genuine connections between artists and fans. Our features are designed to foster meaningful interactions and build sustainable communities around the music you love.

Transparent Analytics

We provide comprehensive, real-time data to help artists understand their audience and optimize their strategy. Knowledge is power, and we believe artists deserve full access to their performance metrics.

Artist-First Discovery

Our discovery algorithms are designed to promote music based on quality and listener engagement, not pay-for-play schemes or corporate priorities. We're creating a level playing field for independent artists.

Privacy Respected

We don't sell user data or track listeners across the web. Our business model is based on fair value exchange between artists and fans, not surveillance capitalism.
