Fan Guide

Welcome to Stamp

This guide will help you make the most of your experience as a music fan on Stamp. We've designed our platform to create meaningful connections between fans and artists, while ensuring that your support directly benefits the creators you love.

Ready to discover new music?

Join thousands of music lovers who are supporting artists directly and discovering their next favorite tracks.

Join the community

Getting Started

1

Create Your Fan Profile

Your profile is your identity in the Stamp community. Personalize it with a photo, bio, and your music preferences to connect with like-minded fans and artists.

Pro Tip

Add your favorite genres and artists to your profile to get personalized recommendations that match your taste.

2

Discover New Music

Explore our curated playlists, community recommendations, and personalized suggestions to find your next favorite artist.

Pro Tip

Check out the "Rising Stars" section to discover emerging artists before they break through to the mainstream.

3

Support Artists You Love

On Stamp, your support goes directly to artists. Choose how you want to contribute, whether through streaming, direct tips, subscriptions, or merchandise purchases.

Pro Tip

Even small contributions make a big difference. A $5 monthly subscription to an artist on Stamp can be worth more than thousands of streams on traditional platforms.

4

Join the Conversation

Engage with artists and other fans through comments, direct messages, and community forums. Your feedback and support mean the world to creators.

Pro Tip

Many artists on Stamp respond personally to comments and messages. Don't be shy about reaching out to tell them what their music means to you.

Ways to Support Artists

Stream Their Music

Every stream on Stamp generates significantly more revenue for artists than traditional platforms. Your listening habits directly support their work.

Subscribe to Artists

Monthly subscriptions provide artists with reliable income and give you access to exclusive content, early releases, and special perks.

Send Direct Support

Tips and one-time payments let you show appreciation for specific tracks or albums that resonate with you.

Buy Merchandise

Purchase physical items like vinyl, CDs, clothing, or digital goods like high-quality downloads and exclusive content.

Share Their Music

Spread the word about artists you love. Each share helps them reach new potential fans and grow their audience.

Engage & Provide Feedback

Comments, likes, and feedback help artists understand what resonates with their audience and improves their visibility on the platform.

Exclusive Fan Benefits

Early Access

Get new music before anyone else. Many artists on Stamp release their work to subscribers days or weeks before public release.

Behind-the-Scenes Content

Enjoy exclusive videos, photos, and stories that show the creative process and personal side of your favorite artists.

Exclusive Events

Get access to virtual listening parties, Q&A sessions, and first dibs on concert tickets when artists announce tours.

High-Quality Downloads

Download music in lossless formats for the best possible listening experience, often available only to supporting fans.

Direct Communication

Many artists offer private messaging or community spaces where they interact directly with their most dedicated supporters.

Special Recognition

Get your name in album credits, receive personalized thank-you messages, or even influence upcoming projects through feedback.
